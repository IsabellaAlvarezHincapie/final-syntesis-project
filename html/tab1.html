Electric energy

is the power an atom´s changed partides have to cause an action to move and object. The movement of electrons from one atom´s to other is what results in electrical energy.

Watt

The standard unit of power in the international system of units (SI), equal to the power in a circuit in with current of on ampere flows across a potencial difference of one volt.

Scatter plot

A Scatter plot uses dots to represents values for two different numeric variables. The position of each dot on the horizontal and vertical axis indicates values for an individual data point.

Linear regression

Is a model of the relationship between two things for predictive analysis. In which one is dependent and the other is independent.

Renewable energy

Renewable energy is energy from natural sources that are replenished at a higher rate than ther are consumed. Sunlight and wind, for example, are such sources that are constantly being replenished.
